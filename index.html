Hello, it's me!

I am a 4th year Computing Science/ Business Joint Major student at Simon Fraser University in Vancouver, Canada.

It excites me to take complex problems and turn them into simple and creative designs, be it graphics or software development. I love the logic and structure of coding and always strive to write elegant and smart code, whether it be C++, Python, HTML, CSS, or Javascript.

When I am not coding or working on cool projects, I volunteer as a technical development coordinator at the Design Lab, work as a fashion model at Vancouver Fashion Week , and I enjoy hiking and exploring more about the beautiful British Columbia.

A fun fact about me is that I traveled to 10 countries in Europe during my 4-month exchange term in Milan, Italy. (See my Photography Gallery!) I am a firm believer in “travel far enough, you may meet yourself”!
